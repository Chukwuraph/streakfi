# StreakFi — UI/UX Design Requirements
**Product:** DeFi Streak Reward Platform powered by Torque Protocol
**Target Chain:** Solana
**Primary DEX:** Raydium

## Design Goals
* **G1: Make streaks feel alive** (Duolingo for DeFi).
* **G2: Surface Torque at every reward moment.**
* **G3: Reduce time-to-first-streak (<60s).**
* **G4: Make sharing irresistible.**

## Visual Specs
* **Palette:** Streak Orange (#F97316), Torque Indigo (#6366F1), Deep Night (#111827).
* **Typography:** Inter (Black/Bold for streaks, Regular for body).
* **Motion:** Odometer-style streak increments, pulsing fire motifs.
